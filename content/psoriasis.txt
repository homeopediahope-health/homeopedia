--- INTRO ---
Psoriasis ek chronic autoimmune skin condition hai jisme skin pe red, raised patches hote hain jiske upar silver-white scales hoti hain. India mein 2-3% population ko affect karta hai — lagbhag 2.8 crore Indians. Homeopathy mein CCRH ke research-backed treatment se 70-78% patients mein significant improvement dekha gaya hai.

--- QUICK FACTS ---
Kya hai: Chronic autoimmune skin condition — body ki immunity galat chal rahi hai, skin cells bahut fast banti hain
India mein kitna common: 2-3% population, approx 2.8 crore Indians
Treatment duration: 4-8 months for significant improvement
CCRH success rate: 70-78% patients mein significant improvement

--- SYMPTOMS ---
Skin Changes:
- Red, raised patches of skin (plaques)
- Silver-white scales on top of patches
- Dry, cracked skin that may bleed
- Patches commonly on elbows, knees, scalp, lower back
- Skin thickening in affected areas

Physical Sensations:
- Intense itching (sometimes severe)
- Burning sensation on skin
- Skin tightness and discomfort
- Soreness when patches are touched
- Stinging during flare-ups

Nail Changes:
- Small pits or dents in nails
- Discolored nails (yellow-brown)
- Thickened or crumbling nails
- Nails separating from nail bed (onycholysis)

Joint Symptoms (Psoriatic Arthritis):
- Swollen, painful joints
- Morning stiffness lasting over 30 minutes
- Swollen fingers or toes (dactylitis)
- Lower back pain

Scalp Symptoms:
- Thick flakes on scalp (looks like dandruff but thicker)
- Itchy scalp with redness
- Hair loss in severe cases
- Scales extending beyond hairline

--- TYPES ---
TYPE: Plaque Psoriasis | 80-90% cases | plaque-psoriasis
BRIEF: Sabse common type. Thick red raised patches with silvery-white scales. Elbows, knees, scalp aur lower back pe hoti hai.
FULL: Plaque psoriasis psoriasis ka sabse common form hai jo 80-90% psoriasis patients mein hota hai. Is type mein skin pe thick, red, raised areas bante hain jinho plaque kehte hain. In patches ke upar silvery-white scales hoti hain. Ye patches typically elbows, knees, lower back aur scalp pe hoti hain lekin body ke kisi bhi hisse pe ho sakti hain. Patches ka size ek chote sikke se lekar bahut badi jagah tak ho sakta hai. Kuch patients mein patches ek doosre se milke bade area ko cover karti hain. Homeopathy mein plaque psoriasis ka treatment patient ki complete constitution ke basis pe hota hai — sirf skin ke symptoms nahi balki patient ki mental state, food preferences, weather sensitivity sab ko dhyan mein liya jata hai.
SYMPTOMS: Thick red raised patches (plaques) on skin | Silvery-white dry scales on patches | Intense itching and burning | Dry cracked skin that bleeds | Patches on elbows, knees, scalp, lower back | Skin thickening over time
TRIGGERS: Stress and anxiety | Alcohol consumption | Smoking | Strep throat or other infections | Skin injury or sunburn | Certain medications (beta-blockers, lithium) | Weather changes | Obesity
TREATMENT: Plaque psoriasis ke liye Sulphur 200 ya 1M sabse commonly indicated medicine hai — khaas kar jab dry, itchy, burning patches hon jo heat se worse hon aur bathing se aggravate karti hon. Graphites kaam aata hai jab thick rough patches hon aur honey-like discharge ho. Arsenicum Album jab burning patches hon aur patient restless aur anxious ho. Constitutional treatment se 4-6 months mein significant improvement milta hai.
WHOGETS: Kisi bhi age mein ho sakta hai. Do peak age groups hain: 15-35 years (early onset) aur 50-60 years (late onset). Men aur women equally affected. Family history hone pe risk 40% tak badh jata hai.
SKINCARE: Daily heavy moisturization — coconut oil ya petroleum jelly | Sirf lukewarm bath lein, hot water avoid karein | Bath ke 3 minute andar moisturizer apply karein | Tight clothing avoid karein | Scratching bilkul nahi — infection risk badh jata hai | 20-30 min gentle sunlight beneficial hai | Neem oil anti-inflammatory hoti hai

TYPE: Scalp Psoriasis | 45-56% cases | scalp-psoriasis
BRIEF: Scalp pe thick scales aur itching. Dandruff jaise dikhta hai lekin bahut zyada thick aur silvery hota hai. Hairline se bahar bhi aata hai.
FULL: Scalp psoriasis ek bahut common type hai jo lagbhag aadhe psoriasis patients ko affect karta hai. Ye sirf scalp tak limited nahi rehti — often forehead, back of neck aur ears ke aas paas tak extend karti hai. Bahut log scalp psoriasis ko simple dandruff samajh lete hain lekin dono mein fark hota hai — dandruff ke flakes white aur oily hote hain jabki scalp psoriasis ke scales silvery-white aur thick hote hain. Scalp psoriasis mein itching bahut severe ho sakti hai. Severe cases mein hair loss bhi ho sakta hai, lekin ye generally temporary hota hai — proper treatment ke baad hair wapas aate hain. Homeopathy mein scalp psoriasis ka treatment bahut effective hota hai kyunki ye root cause ko address karta hai.
SYMPTOMS: Thick silvery-white scales on scalp | Severe itching on scalp | Redness and inflammation | Flaking that looks like heavy dandruff | Scaling beyond hairline on forehead | Hair loss in severe cases | Dry scalp with tightness | Scales on ears and back of neck
TRIGGERS: Stress (major trigger for scalp psoriasis) | Hair products with harsh chemicals | Frequent hair washing with hot water | Infections | Cold dry weather | Hormonal changes
TREATMENT: Graphites 200 scalp psoriasis mein bahut effective hai — khaas kar jab thick rough scales hon aur honey-like discharge ho. Mezereum jab scalp pe thick crusts hon aur bahut intense itching ho. Lycopodium 200 jab scalp psoriasis ke saath digestion issues hon. Oil massage ke saath homeopathy zyada effective hoti hai. Medicated shampoos ke comparison mein homeopathy scalp psoriasis mein better long-term results deta hai.
WHOGETS: Kisi bhi age group mein. Stress-sensitive individuals mein zyada. Women mein menopause ke time worsening dekhi jaati hai. Ye akeli ya plaque psoriasis ke saath ho sakti hai.
SKINCARE: Mild sulfate-free shampoo use karein | Harsh chemicals avoid karein — no hair color, no chemical straightening | Coconut oil ya neem oil scalp massage beneficial hai | Gently comb ke saath scales hatao — forcefully nahi | Soft bristle brush use karein | Sun exposure to scalp beneficial but sunburn avoid karein

TYPE: Guttate Psoriasis | 10-15% cases | guttate-psoriasis
BRIEF: Small drop-shaped spots jo suddenly aate hain. Mostly strep throat infection ke baad trigger hota hai. Children aur young adults mein zyada.
FULL: Guttate psoriasis ka naam Latin word "gutta" se aaya hai jiska matlab hai "drop." Is type mein small, drop-shaped, red spots hote hain jo typically trunk (chest aur back), arms aur legs pe hote hain. Face, ears aur scalp bhi affect ho sakta hai. Ye spots usually plaque psoriasis ke patches se chhote hote hain. Guttate psoriasis often suddenly appear hota hai — aur usually ek strep throat infection ke 2-3 weeks baad. Is type mein pehla episode often resolve ho jaata hai apne aap ya treatment se, lekin future mein plaque psoriasis develop hone ka risk rehta hai. Homeopathy mein guttate psoriasis bahut well respond karta hai treatment ke liye.
SYMPTOMS: Small drop-shaped red spots on body | Spots on chest, back, arms, legs | May have fine silver scales | Sudden onset after infection | Mild to moderate itching | Spots can number in hundreds
TRIGGERS: Strep throat infection (most common trigger) | Upper respiratory infections | Stress | Skin injury | Certain medications | Tonsillitis
TREATMENT: Dulcamara 200 jab guttate psoriasis infection ke baad aaye — khaas kar cold damp weather mein. Rhus Toxicodendron jab spots bahut itchy hon aur movement se better hon. Antimonium Crudum jab thick honey-colored crusts hon. Strep throat ko clear karna bhi important hai. 3-4 months mein good response milti hai guttate psoriasis mein.
WHOGETS: Mainly children (5-15 years) aur young adults (teens to 30s) mein. Men aur women dono mein. Often pehla episode hota hai life mein. 40% cases mein plaque psoriasis future mein develop ho sakti hai.
SKINCARE: Gentle moisturization | Lukewarm bath with oatmeal soak soothing hota hai | Tight synthetic clothes avoid karein | Cotton clothes prefer karein | Scratching avoid karein — secondary infection ho sakta hai

TYPE: Inverse Psoriasis | 2-6% cases | inverse-psoriasis
BRIEF: Skin ke folds mein hoti hai — armpits, groin, under breasts, navel ke aas paas. Smooth red patches, scales nahi hote kyunki moisture rahti hai.
FULL: Inverse psoriasis skin ke folds aur crease areas mein hoti hai — jaise armpits (bagal), groin (kamar ke niche), under breasts, navel (naabhi) ke aas paas, aur genitals ke aas paas. Kyunki ye areas moist aur warm rehte hain, is type mein plaque psoriasis jaise silvery scales nahi hote — instead smooth, shiny, bright red patches hote hain. Ye patches bahut sensitive hote hain aur friction se worse ho jaate hain. Sweating se bhi irritation badh jaati hai. Inverse psoriasis mein fungal ya bacterial infection ka risk bhi zyada rehta hai — jo condition ko aur worse kar sakta hai. Overweight ya obese individuals mein zyada common hai kyunki unke skin folds zyada hote hain. Homeopathy mein is type ka treatment bahut effective hai especially jab lifestyle guidance ke saath combine kiya jaata hai.
SYMPTOMS: Smooth bright red patches in skin folds | No or minimal silvery scales | Intense burning and soreness | Worsens with sweat and friction | Very sensitive to touch | Armpits, groin, under breasts affected | Raw skin feeling
TRIGGERS: Sweating and friction | Overweight aur obesity | Tight synthetic clothing | Hot humid weather | Fungal infections | Stress
TREATMENT: Sulphur 30 ya 200 inverse psoriasis mein effective hai — khaas kar jab heat aur sweating se worse ho. Graphites jab skin folds mein moist eruptions aur cracks hon. Petroleum jab skin bahut raw aur chapped feel ho. Lycopodium jab navel aur groin area mainly affected ho. Breathable clothing aur weight management ke saath treatment zyada effective hoti hai.
WHOGETS: Overweight ya obese individuals mein zyada. Adults mein zyada common. In people with deep skin folds. Often accompanies plaque psoriasis.
SKINCARE: Breathable cotton loose clothing pehno | Skin folds ko dry rakhein | Talcum powder ya arrowroot powder folds mein help karta hai | Synthetic fabrics avoid karein | Sweat absorbing clothes prefer karein | Gently clean and pat dry (rub nahi)

TYPE: Pustular Psoriasis | 5% cases | pustular-psoriasis
BRIEF: Pus-filled bumps (pustules) skin pe hote hain. Infection nahi hai — pus white blood cells hain. Haath aur pair ke talawe pe zyada. Severe form ho sakta hai.
FULL: Pustular psoriasis ek uncommon but serious type hai. Isme skin pe white pus-filled blisters ya pustules hote hain jo red, swollen skin pe hote hain. Ye pustules infection se nahi hain — inme pus aslmein white blood cells hota hai. Ye generally haath ki hatheli (palms) aur pair ke talawe (soles) pe hote hain — ise palmoplantar pustulosis kehte hain. Kabhi kabhi ye body ke wide area ko cover kar sakta hai — jo zyada serious hota hai. Generalized pustular psoriasis ek medical emergency ho sakta hai — isme fever, nausea aur muscle weakness bhi ho sakti hai. Homeopathy mein localized pustular psoriasis bahut well respond karta hai. Generalized type mein pehle allopathic emergency care zaroori hai, uske baad homeopathy se long-term management kiya jaata hai.
SYMPTOMS: White or yellow pus-filled bumps on skin | Surrounding skin red and tender | Bumps on palms and soles mainly | Bumps may merge to form sheets | Burning pain in affected area | Fever in generalized type | Fatigue and weakness | Skin may peel after pustules dry
TRIGGERS: Suddenly stopping steroid medicines | Pregnancy | Infections | Certain medications | Stress | UV light overexposure | Low blood calcium
TREATMENT: Mercurius Solubilis 30 ya 200 pustular psoriasis mein effective hai jab pus-filled eruptions hon aur patient zyada sweat karta ho. Hepar Sulphuricum 30 jab eruptions bahut painful aur sensitive hon. Silica 200 jab slow-healing pustules hon aur weak immunity ho. Kanpur ya generalized type mein pehle medical emergency management, phir homeopathy. 5-6 months ka treatment period.
WHOGETS: Adults mein zyada common. Women mein slightly more common (khaas kar pregnancy-related). Plaque psoriasis patients mein sometimes develop hota hai. Steroid suddenly bandh karne ke baad.
SKINCARE: Pustules ko phodna bilkul nahi | Gentle moisturization | Sterile bandaging if pustules open | Cotton gloves raaton ko agar haath affected ho | Footwear jo pressure reduce kare if feet affected | Cool water soaks soothing hote hain

TYPE: Erythrodermic Psoriasis | 2-3% cases | erythrodermic-psoriasis
BRIEF: Rare but bahut serious type. Body ke 90% se zyada area cover ho jaata hai. Medical emergency hai. Pehle hospital care zaroori hai.
FULL: Erythrodermic psoriasis psoriasis ka sabse rare aur severe form hai. Isme body ki almost poori skin surface — 90% ya zyada — red, inflamed aur peeling ho jaati hai. Skin fire-like red ho jaati hai aur large sheets mein shed hoti hai. Ye condition bahut dangerous hai kyunki isme body apna temperature regulate nahi kar pati, proteins aur fluids bahut zyada lose hote hain, aur infection ka bahut zyada risk rehta hai. Patient ko heart rate fast, body temperature unstable, aur bahut weakness ho sakti hai. Ye ek medical emergency hai — immediate hospitalization zaroori hai. Homeopathy is condition mein sirf ek supporting role play kar sakti hai — pehle conventional medical care absolutely essential hai. Emergency treatment ke baad, constitutional homeopathy relapse prevention mein help kar sakti hai.
SYMPTOMS: Widespread redness covering 90%+ body | Severe skin peeling in large sheets | Intense itching and burning | Fever and chills | Rapid heartbeat | Dehydration | Protein loss | Extreme fatigue
TRIGGERS: Sudden stopping of systemic psoriasis medications | Severe sunburn on existing psoriasis | Infection | Certain medications (antimalarials, lithium) | Alcohol withdrawal
TREATMENT: Ye medical emergency hai — pehle hospitalization aur conventional treatment absolutely essential hai. Emergency stabilization ke baad, homeopathy constitutional treatment relapse prevention mein help karta hai. Arsenicum Album, Sulphur, aur Carcinosinum jaise deep-acting constitutional remedies long-term mein use ki jaati hain. Homeopath ki strict supervision mein treatment hona chahiye.
WHOGETS: Existing plaque psoriasis patients mein more likely. Jo patients abruptly medications stop karte hain. Severe stress ya infection trigger kar sakta hai. Any age, both genders.
SKINCARE: Ye medical emergency hai — self-treatment bilkul nahi. Hospital mein IV fluids, wound care, temperature management. Discharge ke baad gentle moisturization, infection prevention paramount.

TYPE: Nail Psoriasis | 50-80% patients | nail-psoriasis
BRIEF: Nails mein pitting, ridges, yellow-brown color. Nails kaafi thick ho sakti hain ya nail bed se alag ho sakti hain. Akele ya skin psoriasis ke saath.
FULL: Nail psoriasis bahut common hai — psoriasis ke 50-80% patients mein kuch na kuch nail involvement hota hai. Ye toenails aur fingernails dono ko affect kar sakta hai. Nail psoriasis ko often fungal nail infection samjh liya jaata hai kyunki dono mein nail ka discoloration aur thickening hota hai. Diagnosis mein ye fark jaroori hai kyunki treatment alag hota hai. Nail psoriasis mein nail bed ke andar psoriatic changes hote hain jo nails ke appearance aur texture ko affect karte hain. Ye condition cosmetically bothersome hoti hai aur kuch patients mein pain bhi hota hai. Nail psoriasis psoriatic arthritis ka early sign bhi ho sakta hai. Homeopathy mein nail psoriasis ka treatment patient ke full constitution ke basis pe hota hai aur good results milte hain.
SYMPTOMS: Pitting (small dents) in nail surface | Onycholysis (nail separating from nail bed) | Yellow-brown or white discoloration | Thickening and crumbling of nails | Ridges on nail surface | Subungual hyperkeratosis (buildup under nail) | Splinter hemorrhages (small blood lines)
TRIGGERS: Same as skin psoriasis — stress, infections, injury to nail area | Nail trauma or repetitive pressure | Ill-fitting shoes for toenails
TREATMENT: Silica 200 ya 1M nail psoriasis mein bahut effective hai — khaas kar jab nails brittle, thickened aur crumbling hon. Thuja Occidentalis 200 jab nail deformity aur warts saath hon. Antimonium Crudum jab nails thick aur discolored hon aur brittle ho. Treatment mein 6-9 months lag sakte hain kyunki nails slowly grow karti hain. Constitutional treatment nail psoriasis mein best results deta hai.
WHOGETS: Psoriasis patients mein se 50-80% ko hoti hai. Psoriatic arthritis patients mein almost always nail involvement hota hai. Men mein slightly more common. Any age group.
SKINCARE: Nails short rakhein | Nail cutting ke time careful rahein | Gentle moisturizer nails ke aas paas apply karein | Rubber gloves washing-up ke time | Water mein zyada der tak haath nahi dibbond | Nail polish remover (acetone-based) avoid karein | Comfortable shoes pehno agar toenails affected hain

TYPE: Psoriatic Arthritis | 30% risk in psoriasis patients | psoriatic-arthritis
BRIEF: Joint pain + skin psoriasis. Morning stiffness, swollen fingers. Skin psoriasis se pehle, baad mein ya saath ho sakta hai. Early treatment bahut zaroori hai.
FULL: Psoriatic arthritis ek inflammatory arthritis hai jo psoriasis patients mein develop ho sakta hai. Ye sirf joints ka psoriasis nahi — ye ek alag inflammatory condition hai jo joints ko affect karti hai. Psoriasis ke 30% patients mein kabhi na kabhi psoriatic arthritis develop hoti hai. Ye skin symptoms se pehle, saath mein, ya baad mein develop ho sakta hai. Psoriatic arthritis ke symptoms bahut varied hote hain — chhoti finger joints se lekar spine tak koi bhi joint affected ho sakta hai. Morning stiffness, swollen sausage-like fingers (dactylitis), aur heel pain (enthesitis) is condition ke characteristic features hain. Early treatment bahut zaroori hai kyunki untreated psoriatic arthritis permanent joint damage kar sakti hai. Homeopathy mein psoriatic arthritis ka treatment dual-benefit hai — dono skin symptoms aur joint inflammation ko address karta hai.
SYMPTOMS: Joint pain and swelling | Morning stiffness lasting 30+ minutes | Sausage-like swollen fingers or toes (dactylitis) | Heel pain or foot pain (enthesitis) | Lower back pain and stiffness | Reduced range of motion | Fatigue | Nail changes (pitting, separation) | Eye inflammation in some cases
TRIGGERS: Stress | Infections | Physical trauma to joints | Smoking | Obesity | Certain medications
TREATMENT: Rhus Toxicodendron 200 ya 1M psoriatic arthritis mein classic remedy hai — jab morning stiffness bahut zyada ho, movement se better ho aur rest se worse. Bryonia jab joints swollen, hot aur movement se bahut worse hon. Causticum jab contractures aur stiffness chronic ho. Actaea Spicata jab small joints (fingers, wrists) zyada affected hon. Combination of constitutional treatment aur specific joint remedies best results deta hai. 6-9 months ka treatment period often needed.
WHOGETS: 30% psoriasis patients mein develop hota hai. Usually 30-50 years ki age mein. Men aur women equally. Family history hone pe risk badh jaata hai. Nail psoriasis aur severe plaque psoriasis mein risk zyada.
SKINCARE: Joints ko warm rakhein, especially mornings mein | Warm compresses soothing hote hain | Excessive joint strain avoid karein | Gentle stretching aur yoga beneficial | Swimming best exercise hai — low impact | Ergonomic support tools agar work pe joint stress ho

--- MEDICINES ---
Sulphur | 30/200/1M | Best for: Plaque psoriasis with burning, itching worse from heat
Indications:
- Dry, itchy, burning patches — especially on elbows and knees
- Worse from heat and warmth of bed
- Aggravation from bathing
- Offensive body odor
- Unhealthy dirty-looking skin
- Worse in winter and spring

Arsenicum Album | 30/200 | Best for: Psoriasis with anxiety, burning worse at night
Indications:
- Dry scaly skin with intense burning sensation
- Restlessness worse at night (1-3 AM)
- Worse in cold weather
- Anxiety and fear about health
- Better from warm applications despite burning
- Patches worse on right side

Graphites | 30/200 | Best for: Scalp psoriasis, moist eruptions, thick rough patches
Indications:
- Honey-like sticky discharge from cracks
- Thick rough hard patches
- Scalp involvement with matted hair
- Overweight patients with skin issues
- Cracks in skin folds

Petroleum | 30/200 | Best for: Winter-worsening psoriasis, deep cracks
Indications:
- Deep painful cracks especially on hands and feet
- Winter aggravation — much worse in cold
- Bleeding fissures that are slow to heal
- Rough, hard, leathery skin
- Better in summer

Rhus Toxicodendron | 30/200 | Best for: Psoriasis with joint pain (psoriatic arthritis)
Indications:
- Joint involvement with skin symptoms
- Better from movement and warmth
- Morning stiffness — worse on first movement then improves
- Restless patients who can't stay in one position
- Worse in cold damp weather

Mezereum | 30/200 | Best for: Scalp psoriasis with thick crusts
Indications:
- Thick leathery crusts on scalp
- Itching violently, especially at night
- Cold air relieves itching
- Burning after scratching
- Offensive discharge under crusts

Silica | 200/1M | Best for: Nail psoriasis, weak immune system
Indications:
- Brittle, thickened, discolored nails
- Nails crumbling and separating
- Weak constitution, low stamina
- Slow healing of skin
- Profuse offensive sweat

--- LIFESTYLE TIPS ---
Morning Walk:
30-45 minute morning walk, ideally 6:30-7:30 AM. Natural sunlight provides Vitamin D jo psoriasis mein deficient hota hai. Walking se stress hormones reduce hote hain. Vitamin D skin cell growth regulate karta hai — psoriasis mein skin cells bahut fast grow karti hain. Sunlight phototherapy ka free natural form hai.

Yoga aur Pranayama:
Sarvangasana (shoulder stand), Matsyasana (fish pose), Pawanmuktasana (wind-relieving pose) roz karein — ye skin ko blood supply better karti hain. Anulom-Vilom pranayama 10-15 minute daily — stress significantly reduce hota hai. Kapalbhati 5 minute — detoxification mein help. Yoga CCRH data mein 60-70% flares jo stress-triggered hain unhe reduce karta hai.

Skincare Routine:
Lukewarm bath only — hot water psoriasis ke liye bahut harmful hai. Bath ke 3 minute ke andar moisturizer apply karein — LOCK karo moisture. Natural oils — coconut oil, neem oil, castor oil — chemical-free best options hain. Petroleum jelly (Vaseline) thick moisturizer ke roop mein raat ko apply karein. Gentle, fragrance-free soap use karein — Dove ya Cetaphil jaisa.

Stress Management:
10 minute daily meditation — even simple deep breathing. 7-8 hours quality sleep zaroori hai — sleep deprivation inflammation badhata hai. Journaling before bed — thoughts release karein. CCRH data: 60-70% psoriasis flares stress-triggered hain isliye stress management treatment ka integral part hai. Social support aur talking to friends bhi stress reduce karta hai.

Sunlight Therapy:
20-30 minute moderate sunlight daily — especially on affected patches. Early morning (8-10 AM) ya late afternoon (4-5 PM) best time. Avoid peak sun (12-3 PM). --- FAQs ---
Q: Psoriasis kya hoti hai? Iska kya matlab hai?
A: Psoriasis ek chronic autoimmune skin disease hai jisme body ki immune system apni hi skin cells pe galat attack karti hai. Iska result yeh hota hai ki skin cells bahut tezi se grow karti hain — normal skin cells 28-30 din mein replace hoti hain, psoriasis mein sirf 3-7 din mein. Ye extra cells surface pe collect hoti hain aur red patches with silvery scales banti hain. Ye koi infection nahi hai — ye body ki immunity ka malfunction hai.

Q: Kya psoriasis contagious hai? Touch karne se failti hai?
A: Bilkul nahi. Psoriasis contagious nahi hai — na touch karne se, na kapde share karne se, na swimming pool se, na khaana saath khaane se. Ye autoimmune condition hai, koi infection nahi. Psoriasis wale vyakti ke saath bilkul normal behavior karein — unhe embarrass mat karein. Shaadi, social gatherings, swimming — kuch bhi restricted nahi hai psoriasis wale ke liye socially.

Q: Kya psoriasis hereditary hai? Family mein hai toh kya mujhe bhi hogi?
A: Psoriasis mein genetic component hota hai — 40% cases mein family history hoti hai. Lekin genetics alone psoriasis nahi karta — environmental triggers zaroori hain. Agar parents mein psoriasis hai toh bachon mein 10-25% chance hota hai. Siblings mein 15-20% chance. Lekin bahut log jinke family mein psoriasis hai unhe kabhi nahi hoti. Environmental factors — stress, diet, infections, smoking — trigger karte hain.

Q: Psoriasis aur eczema mein kya fark hai?
A: Dono alag conditions hain. Psoriasis mein red patches pe thick silvery scales hote hain, elbows-knees-scalp pe zyada, chronic aur relapsing course. Eczema mein scales nahi hote, skin weepy/oozing hoti hai, inner elbows aur behind knees pe zyada, allergies ke saath related, often childhood mein shuru. Psoriasis autoimmune hai, eczema mostly allergic. Diagnosis ke liye doctor se milein.

Q: Psoriasis kitne types ki hoti hai?
A: 8 main types hain: (1) Plaque psoriasis — sabse common, 80-90% cases; (2) Scalp psoriasis — 45-56% patients; (3) Guttate psoriasis — drop-shaped spots, 10-15%; (4) Inverse psoriasis — skin folds mein, 2-6%; (5) Pustular psoriasis — pus-filled bumps, 5%; (6) Erythrodermic psoriasis — rare, severe, 2-3%; (7) Nail psoriasis — 50-80% patients mein; (8) Psoriatic arthritis — joint involvement, 30% risk.

Q: Doctor ke paas kab jaana chahiye?
A: Immediately agar: skin suddenly bahut widespread red ho jaaye (erythrodermic), joint pain shuru ho, skin crack karke bahut bleed kare, steroid suddenly band karne ke baad severe flare aaye. Routine mein: agar itching aur discomfort daily life affect kar raha hai, agar already months se self-treating ho bina improvement ke, agar nails bahut deformed ho rahe hain.

CCRH ke data mein 70-78% patients mein significant improvement hai. Bahut patients 5+ years flare-free rehte hain proper treatment aur lifestyle se. "Cure" se zyada practical goal hai: zyada kam flares, milder episodes, better quality of life.

Severity ke hisab se vary karta hai — mild psoriasis 3 months mein respond kar sakti hai, severe chronic cases mein 12+ months lag sakte hain.

Q: Stress aur psoriasis ka kya connection hai?
A: Bahut strong connection hai. CCRH data mein 60-70% psoriasis flares stress-triggered hote hain. Stress se cortisol aur inflammatory cytokines increase hote hain jo psoriasis worsen karte hain. Stress management — yoga, meditation, exercise — treatment ka integral part hai. Mental health pe attention dena zaruri hai — 30% psoriasis patients mein depression bhi co-exist karta hai.

Q: Swimming kar sakte hain psoriasis mein?
A: Moderate pool swimming OK hai lekin precautions chahiye. Pool chlorine excessive hone pe irritate kar sakta hai — after swimming turant shower aur moisturize karein. Sea water (salt water) psoriasis mein actually beneficial ho sakta hai. Infection risk ke liye: open pustular lesions mein swimming avoid karein. Overall, light exercise ke roop mein swimming bahut acha hai — stress reduce karta hai jo flares kam karta hai.

Q: Psoriatic arthritis ko kaise samjhein? Kya ye serious hai?
A: Ye serious hai aur early treatment zaruri hai. Psoriasis ke 30% patients mein develop ho sakti hai. Q: Winter mein psoriasis kyun zyada badhti hai?
