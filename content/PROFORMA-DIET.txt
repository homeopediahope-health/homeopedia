TIPS FOR FILLING THIS PROFORMA:
- Har meal me season ke hisab se change kar sakte ho (summer/winter)
- Indian foods use karo: daliya, poha, khichdi, sabzi-roti, etc.
- Jis food se is disease me problem ho vo avoid list me zaroor likho
- Weekly chart me variety rakho — same food baar baar mat likho
- Agar patient vegetarian hai to vegi options do (majority Indian patients)
- Mid morning aur Shaam me fruits, herbal tea, nuts jaise light options best hai
